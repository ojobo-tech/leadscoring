# Project Brief

## Project Title
AI Lead Prioritization and Engagement Scoring System

## Business Problem
Sales teams spend too much time on low-value leads and too little time on the leads most likely to respond and move forward. They need a practical way to rank leads by priority, engagement, and follow-up urgency so they can focus their effort where it matters most.

## Target User
SMB sales representatives, sales managers, and business owners who need a clear way to prioritize outreach and manage sales activity.

## Product Goal
Build a decision-support system that scores leads based on priority and engagement, explains why the lead was ranked that way, and helps the sales team focus on the best opportunities first.

## Business Value
The system should help the client save time, improve lead prioritization, reduce guesswork, and make sales outreach more consistent and effective.

## Scope
The first version will focus on lead prioritization and engagement scoring using lead, task, owner, and activity data. The system will produce a priority score, an engagement score, and a recommended action for each lead. Later versions can expand into churn, opportunity scoring, or forecasting modules.

## Success Criteria
The project is successful if it can rank leads in a way that is useful to sales teams, surface the most active or urgent opportunities, explain the score clearly, and present the result in a simple dashboard or decision workspace.

## One-Sentence Summary
A lead prioritization and engagement scoring system that helps sales teams focus on the best opportunities first.
